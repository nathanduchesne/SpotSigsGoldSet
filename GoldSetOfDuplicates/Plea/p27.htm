William makes press plea over Kate
4:42pm Tue 9 Jan 07
Prince William wants "more than anything" for the paparazzi to stop harassing his girlfriend Kate Middleton, his aides have revealed.

Clarence House said the second-in-line to the throne wanted to see an end to the persistent attention Miss Middleton is facing from photographers.

Lawyers for Miss Middleton, who turned 25 on Tuesday, are said to be attempting to use persuasion rather than legal action to help protect her.
