The Crocodile Hunter's final documentary airs tonight

UNDATED Crocodile Hunter Steve Irwin's final documentary airs tonight on the Discovery Channel.

"Ocean's Deadliest" includes no footage from the day last September when Irwin was fatally stabbed by a stingray on Australia's Great Barrier Reef.

Several Irwin documentaries are airing today and tonight on Discovery, including a special on Irwin's life and legacy, called: "Crikey, What An Adventure."

Meanwhile, a talking "Crocodile Hunter" Steve Irwin action figure that has his recorded voice reciting his signature phrase will go on sale nationwide next month.

The toy-maker K-and-M International was ready to stop the launch of the action figure after Irwin died but his widow, Terri, wanted to go ahead with it.
