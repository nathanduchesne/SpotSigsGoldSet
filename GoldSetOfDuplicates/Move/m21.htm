Diaz back home with mom

 
January 17, 2007
 
COMMENT ON THIS STORY

 
CAMERON DIAZ plans to move back in with her parents to get over her break-up with JUSTIN TIMBERLAKE.

The actress will stay at mum Billie and dad Emilio’s place for some home comfort. Bless her.

At the moment, Cameron’s stuff is still at the house she shares with Justin in Los Angeles.

But she has barely been back there since before Christmas when the couple had a huge row.

Things came to a head over their different festive plans.

Cameron headed off to spend the holiday skiing with her family in Vail, Colorado.

Meanwhile, Justin went back to his relatives in Tennessee.

Now Cameron has told friends she is going to take some time out and move back in with her folks.

A source said: “The split with Justin is totally amicable so there’s no rush.

“But they don’t want to be living in the same house any more.


Split ... Justin

“While Cameron looks for her own place, she wants to go home.

“Her parents also live near Los Angeles, in Long Beach.

“That’s where she is going to move her stuff while she figures out what to do next.”

Cameron and Justin decided to call it a day after a four-year relationship.

They issued a statement confirming the split at the start of this year. It said: “It has always been our preference not to comment on the status of our relationship, but, out of respect for the time we’ve spent together, we feel compelled to do so now.

“We have, in fact, ended our romantic relationship, and have done so mutually and as friends, with continued love and respect for one another.”

The pair even brushed shoulders again at Monday night’s Golden Globes. And they both headed to the same aftershow party — thrown by Warner Brothers.

Cameron is a California girl through and through.

She was born in San Diego, but her family moved north to Long Beach when she was a kid.
