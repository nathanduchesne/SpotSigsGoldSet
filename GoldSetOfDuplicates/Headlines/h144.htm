Wed, January 10, 2007

Life turns paparnasty for royal girlfriend

UPDATED: 2007-01-10 00:58:58 MST


By AP

LONDON -- She makes headlines for getting a parking ticket, newspapers comment on her clothes, and her 25th birthday yesterday generated a media storm.

A frenzy is building around Kate Middleton, fuelled, perhaps, by a large dose of wishful thinking from the media and public.

Pictures of the brunette fashion buyer, who has dated William since both were university students, fetch thousands of dollars.

Paparazzi follow her to work, on shopping trips and on nights out with the prince.

British bookmakers William Hill say it is so certain William will pop the question, it stopped taking bets on whether the pair will get engaged: Only on the date.

Middleton, unlike most previous royal consorts, is middle-class: Her father worked for an airline and her mother was a flight attendant.

Middleton's lawyers have complained to newspaper editors, requesting she be left alone, and British newspapers have recently reported she is to be given a guard from the police royal protection squad.
