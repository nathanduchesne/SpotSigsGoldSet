Lindsay Lohan Goes Back To Work

Rep Says Actress Is On Set Of Latest Film "I Know Who Killed Me"

 Slideshow: Stars In Rehab

 Slideshow: "It Girls" Gone Bad

(CBS) It's back to work for Lindsay Lohan. The actress' rep confirmed to The ShowBuzz Friday that she is back on the set of her latest film, "I Know Who Killed Me."

Chris Sivertson is directing the thriller, also starring Julia Ormond and Kenya Moore. According to a report from People magazine, Lohan's scenes are scheduled to start filming Friday.

The actress entered the posh Wonderland Center in Los Angeles last week.

"I have made a proactive decision to take care of my personal health. I appreciate your well wishes and ask that you please respect my privacy at this time," the 20-year-old actress said in a statement at the time.

"People were getting a bit antsy about what the status was, so it's pretty thrilling," a source tells People about getting back to work. "As soon as Lindsay was cleared, they started everything back up again."

"As far as I know, she'll be back on schedule and working a full day," a source told the magazine. A rep for the movie said 13-14 hours a day is the norm.

She will continue her outpatient therapy, Entertainment Tonight reports.

Production for the film had been on hold for Lohan's appendix surgery on Jan. 4, the report said.
