Lindsay Lohan Back to Work

Last Update: 1/26/2007 2:00:07 PM

Click here to watch Lindsay Lohan's video "Over"

After a week in rehab, Lindsay Lohan is back filming her current movie, 'I Know Who Killed Me.'

The 20-year-old actress/singer checked herself into the Wonderland Center in Los Angeles last week.

Her doctor gave her the okay to resume shooting on the thriller, in which she plays a stripper suffering from dual personalities after a brutal kidnapping.

Lohan will still receive treatment at night while she films the movie during the day.
