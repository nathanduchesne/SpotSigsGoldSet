Person shot at high school in Washington state

POSTED: 11:33 a.m. EST, January 3, 2007
Adjust font size:

TACOMA, Washington (AP) -- One person was shot Wednesday morning at a Tacoma, Washington, high school, and the school was being evacuated, police said.

It wasn't immediately clear what had happened, or if the shooter was still inside the building, police spokesman Mark Fulghum said.

He said police responded to Foss High School shortly after the 7:30 a.m. shooting and were evacuating students. Details on the person shot were not immediately released.
