Judge: Saddam To Be Executed Today or Tomorrow
Last Edited: Friday, 29 Dec 2006, 12:25 PM CST
Created: Friday, 29 Dec 2006, 10:56 AM CST
Credit: MyFox
By The Associated Press

BAGHDAD, Iraq -- Saddam Hussein will be executed by Saturday at the latest, an Iraqi judge said Friday.

Lawyers representing him said the condemned former leader is no longer in U.S. custody.

He was due to be handed over to Iraqi custody before his execution.
