Cameron Diaz Temporarily Moves In With Parents

January 17, 2007 7:09 a.m. EST

Maira Oliveira - All Headline News Reporter

Los Angeles, CA (BANG) - A mother's love always helps heal a broken heart.

Cameron Diaz is moving back in with her mother, to get over her break-up from Justin Timberlake.

"The Holiday" actress has moved out of the Los Angeles home she shared with the "SexyBack" singer and is staying with her parents Billie and Emilio just a short drive away in Long Beach.

A source told Britain's The Sun newspaper, "The split with Justin is totally amicable so there's no rush. But they don't want to be living in the same house any more. While Cameron looks for her own place, she wants to go home. Her parents also live near Los Angeles, in Long Beach. That's where she is going to move her stuff while she figures out what to do next."

The couple - who started dating in 2003 - are believed to have split following a huge fight over their plans for the Christmas holidays.

The 34-year-old joined her family skiing in Colorado, while Justin traveled back home to Tennessee.

A joint statement issued last Thursday confirmed the pair had decided to go their separate ways.

It said, "We have, in fact, ended our romantic relationship, and have done so mutually and as friends, with continued love and respect for one another."
