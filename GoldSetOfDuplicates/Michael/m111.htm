Michael Jordan, Wife File For Divorce

POSTED: 4:13 pm CST December 29, 2006
UPDATED: 4:34 pm CST December 29, 2006
Email This Story | Print This Story
CHICAGO -- Former NBA great Michael Jordan and his wife, Juanita, filed for divorce Friday after 17 years of marriage.

The couple issued a statement through their lawyers saying they mutually and amicably decided to end the marriage, adding that a judgment for dissolution of their marriage was entered Friday.

Juanita Jordan previously filed for divorce in January 2002, but withdrew her petition a month later when the couple announced they were attempting a reconciliation. Jordan said in her previous divorce petition that attempts to reconcile their marriage had failed and future ones "would be impractical and not in the best interests of the family."

Michael Jordan currently is part-owner of the Charlotte Bobcats. He led the Bulls to six NBA titles and was a five-time league MVP.

The couple has three children.
