    Diaz to move back in with parents
    BY: WENN   |   Wednesday, January 17, 2007
    ADVERTISEMENT
    Cameron Diaz will move back in with her parents as she gets over her split with Justin Timberlake - according to British newspaper reports.

    The couple split at the start of the year (07), and THE MASK beauty has decided she needs some home comforts while she gets over the break up.

    A close friend of the Hollywood actress tells British newspaper The Sun: "While Cameron looks for her own place, she wants to go home.

    "Her parents also live near Los Angeles, in Long Beach. That's where she is going to move her stuff while she figures out what to do next."
