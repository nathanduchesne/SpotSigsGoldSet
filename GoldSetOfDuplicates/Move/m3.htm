Diaz To Move Back In With Parents

Jan 17, 2007 - World Entertainment News Network

Cameron Diaz will move back in with her parents as she gets over her split with Justin Timberlake - according to British newspaper reports.

The couple split at the start of the year, and "The Mask" beauty has decided she needs some home comforts while she gets over the break up.
