TACOMA, Wash. Jan 3, 2007 (AP)— One person was shot Wednesday morning at a Tacoma high school, and the school was being evacuated, police said.

It wasn't immediately clear what had happened or if the shooter was still inside the building, police spokesman Mark Fulghum said.

He said police responded to Foss High School shortly after the 7:30 a.m. shooting and were evacuating students. The age of the person shot was not immediately released.
