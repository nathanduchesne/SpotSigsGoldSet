Home sweet home for Diaz
Wednesday, January 17, 2007

Cameron Diaz is making plans to move back with her parents after her split from Justin Timberlake.

The actress will move to her parents home in Long Beach, near Los Angeles.

Currently Diaz's belongings are still at the address she shared with Timberlake before Christmas.

The couple had a huge row over their differing festive plans and she has not been back since.

The singer went to visit relatives in Tennessee while Diaz went ski-ing with her family in Colorado.

A source said: "The split with Justin is totally amicable so there's no rush.

"But they don't want to be living in the same house anymore."

In a joint statement at the beginning of the year the couple said: "It has always been our preference not to comment on the status of our relationship, but, out of respect for the time we've spent together, we feel compelled to do so now."

The couple met briefly at a Golden Globes party, reported The Sun.
