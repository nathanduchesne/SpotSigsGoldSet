Fox subpoenas YouTube over pirated '24' and 'Simpsons' shows

Last Update: 1/25/2007 5:24:47 PM

Save/Share Story
diggreddit
newsvineGoogle
del.icio.usYahoo!
NEW YORK (Dow Jones/AP) - Twentieth Century Fox has subpoenaed the video-sharing Web site YouTube to find out the identity of a user who uploaded four episodes of the T-V show "24" ahead of their regularly scheduled airing.

The subpoena requests that YouTube disclose information sufficient to identify the subscriber so that Fox can stop the infringing immediately.

The subscriber also uploaded 12 episodes of the Simpsons.
