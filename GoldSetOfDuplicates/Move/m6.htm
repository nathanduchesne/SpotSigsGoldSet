Cameron's plan to get over JT

17/01/2007

Cameron Diaz is said to be moving back home with her parents in an attempt to get over her ex, Justin Timberlake.

The stunning actress shared a home with Justin but following their recent split has decided to turn to her folks for some home comforts.

According to the Sun newspaper, Cameron is moving her things to her parents' house in Long Beach, near Los Angeles.

"The split with Justin is totally amicable so there's no rush," a source told the tabloid. "But they don't want to be living in the same house any more."

The A-list couple were reported as having a large fight before Christmas, but a joint statement released by Cameron's agent claimed that the split was entirely amicable.

"It has always been our preference not to comment on the status of our relationship, but, out of respect for the time we've spent together, we feel compelled to do so now, in light of recent speculation and the number of inaccurate stories that are being reported by the media," the statement said.

"We have, in fact, ended our romantic relationship and have done so mutually and as friends, with continued love and respect for one another."

While Cameron is busy moving house media reports claim that the Sexyback singer has been dating the actress Scarlett Johansson although no confirmation has come from the JT camp.
