Newspaper group shuns Kate snaps

Last updated:Tuesday 9-Jan-2007 18:41:21

Efforts to prevent paparazzi harassment of Prince William's girlfriend Kate Middleton have gained some ground.

One major newspaper group introduced a self-imposed ban on using paparazzi pictures of Miss Middleton.

Royal aides revealed Prince William wants "more than anything" for photographers to stop harassing his long-term girlfriend, who ran the gauntlet of media outside her home again on Tuesday, her 25th birthday.
