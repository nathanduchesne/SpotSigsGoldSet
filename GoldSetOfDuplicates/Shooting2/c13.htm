Student Shot To Death At Washington High School

January 3, 2007 12:47 p.m. EST

William Macklin - All Headline News Staff Reporter

Tacoma, WA (AHN) - Police are searching for the gunman who shot and killed a student at a high school early Wednesday, authorities said.

Police spokesman Mark Fulghum told reporters that the shooting occurred around 7:30 am as the student was walking in a hallway at Foss High School. No one else was hurt, police said.

Few details are known about the attack and police have not released the age or name of the slain student. Police believe the shooter may also be a student.

Wednesday was the first day back for students at the school following winter break. Students were evacuated and school officials said classes would be canceled for the rest of the day.
