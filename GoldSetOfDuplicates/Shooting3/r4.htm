 US high school rocked by shooting

    January 04 2007 at 01:13AM

Seattle - A gunman shot and killed a male student at a high school in Tacoma, Washington, before the start of classes on Wednesday, a police spokesperson said.

Police had a suspect in custody after the shooter fled the scene, according to Tacoma Police spokesperson Mark Fulghum.

Police did not have a motive for the shooting, but the suspect and victim were students at Foss High School.

The suspect was picked up a few miles (km) away from the school and police would not disclose the age or name of the shooter or victim.

School officials evacuated students from the campus and canceled classes for the day. It was the first day of classes at the high school after a holiday break.
