LG to unveil world first dual-format DVD player
www.chinaview.cn 2007-01-04 13:38:30

A LG's DVD player.(file photo)
Photo Gallery >>>

    BEIJING, Jan. 4 (Xinhuanet) -- LG Electronics plans to launch a DVD player that will play both warring high-definition DVD formats soon, according to a LG press release issued Thursday.

    The company announced that the first dual-format high-definition disc player will be unveiled at next week's Consumer Electronics Show in Las Vegas. Details, including pricing and availability, will become available then.

    The new DVD player will play discs in the HD DVD format, which is backed by a consortium headed by Toshiba Corp., as well as the rival Blu-ray format, backed by a group led by Sony Corp.

    The two DVD formats have been battling for market share since being introduced in 2006. Both are expected to get a boost this year as more studios release films in the formats and more players become available.

    But the formats are incompatible, forcing consumers to choose. Studios and electronics makers have said the resulting format war would delay widespread adoption of high-def DVDs, which contain sharper images and extra features not possible with standard definition disc.

    LG, based in South Korea, said it expects its dual-format player to "end the confusion and inconvenience of competing high-definition disc formats."
