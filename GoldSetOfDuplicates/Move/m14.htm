Cameron Diaz moves back home

17th January 2007 12:30:02

Cameron Diaz is moving back in with her parents, to get over her split from Justin Timberlake.

'The Holiday' actress has moved out of the Los Angeles home she shared with the 'SexyBack' singer and is staying with her parents Billie and Emilio just a short drive away in Long Beach.

A source told Britain's The Sun newspaper: "The split with Justin is totally amicable so there's no rush.

"But they don't want to be living in the same house any more. While Cameron looks for her own place, she wants to go home.

"Her parents also live near Los Angeles, in Long Beach. That's where she is going to move her stuff while she figures out what to do next."

The couple - who started dating in 2003 - are believed to have split following a huge row over their plans for the Christmas period.
